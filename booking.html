★ Questions ★

Before you book

How far in advance should I book?
Two to four weeks is ideal for events, longer for keynotes and multi-day productions. Last-minute? Ask anyway, the line is always on.
Does he travel outside Nairobi?
Yes. East Africa and worldwide. Travel and accommodation are arranged per engagement.
Can he host in Kiswahili?
Fluently. English, Kiswahili, or both inside the same show. Sheng on request for the right room.
What's included in the fee?
Preparation, run-of-show alignment, and the live hosting itself. Custom scripting, co-hosts, and production add-ons are quoted separately.
How do I pay?
M-Pesa, card, or bank transfer. A deposit confirms the date, the balance settles before or on the day.
