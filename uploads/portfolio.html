About Me

Built for both sides of the camera

I've spent the last five years doing two things simultaneously that most people treat as separate careers — working as a broadcast journalist and TV host, and building AI-powered digital products.

Right now I co-host Urban News on StarTimes Channel 430 to 2M+ weekly viewers while running all digital strategy for PPP TV Kenya. Outside of broadcasting, I'm shipping real products from my GitHub — including an autonomous news station that publishes to Instagram and Facebook daily with no human input.

I grew up in Kakamega, Western Kenya. I graduated as Valedictorian from TIBS. I was trained at Citizen TV. I've reported in the field, sub-edited a magazine, hosted drive-time radio, emceed university concerts, and built SaaS tools — often in the same month.

I'm the founder of Urban Tour, co-host of The Nairobi Podcast, and author of the memoir Born Broke, Built Loud.
