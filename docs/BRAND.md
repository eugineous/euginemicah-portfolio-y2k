## II · Voice & Tone

The register is **funny but serious**. Confident without bragging, warm without being soft. Trevor Noah opens. Kevin Hart admits a flaw and makes you laugh. Will Smith pivots from a joke to a gut punch in one breath.

### Rules

1. **First person where personal** (Story, About, fan zone). **Third person where promotional** (Press, Booking).
2. **Short sentences. One-line sentences live alone.** Then breathe.
3. **Lead with the scar, close with the win.** Never the reverse.
4. **Specific, never generic.** Not "humble beginnings" — "a kerosene lamp doing a streetlight's job."
5. **No corporate filler.** Kill "passionate about," "leveraging synergies," "results-driven."
6. **No em-dashes or en-dashes anywhere.** Use commas, periods, or start a new sentence.
7. **Confidence, not arrogance.** "I built this" is fine. "I'm the best in Africa" is not.
8. **End big sections with a hook.** One sentence that tees up the next.

### Lexicon

- **Use:** loud, built, hustle, scars, dust, mic, gang, studio, desk, room, on the record.
- **Avoid:** synergy, passionate, dynamic, innovative (as filler), cutting-edge, journey (overused).

## III · Type System

| Role        | Family            | Use                                                      |
|-------------|-------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| Display     | **Bodoni Moda**   | Hero names, section titles, big page intros, metrics      |
| Editorial   | **EB Garamond**   | Body copy, long-form, italic standfirsts, descriptions    |
| Small caps  | EB Garamond + smcp| Eyebrows, labels, nav, captions, Roman numerals           |
| Script      | **Pinyon Script** | Soft flourishes — signatures, "eugine.", contact-page sign|
| Sans (rare) | Inter             | Forms, utility, fallback                                  |

**Weights in use:** Bodoni 500 (regular display), 600/700 (heavy), italic 500. EB Garamond 400 body, 600 labels.

**Tracking:** all-caps labels at `0.32em`. Display headlines at `-0.02em` to `-0.04em` for editorial confidence.

  --oxblood-2   #6e1a20   variant

  --crimson     #9a1c25   appears in his red crown wordmark

**Rules:**

- **Default page = warm noir.** Cream sections punctuate. Oxblood is a once-or-twice-a-page accent.
- **No pink. No magenta. Ever.**
- Hover states warm to ivory or gold.
- Gold rules sit at 1px, opacity 0.5–0.7 on dark.

## V · Photography

| Use case                | Treatment                                   |
|-------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Hero backgrounds        | Studio plate with mix-blend luminosity, 18% opacity |
| Editorial plates        | 4:5 ratio, sepia 0.18, saturate 0.85        |
| Press portraits         | Circular crop, formal black-suit            |
| Inline thumbnails       | Same sepia treatment, hover restores colour |

Every plate sits inside a 16px inset 1px gold border. The frame is the signature.

**Subject inventory (current):**
