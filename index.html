★ The Story ★

A boy from Lugari.
A studio in Nairobi.

Born in Kisumu on Christmas Eve, raised on a mud floor in Kakamega, where a kerosene lamp did a streetlight's job.

Twenty-four years later the country watches him on PPP TV at 7:30, and he still introduces himself the same way. The shows are real. The numbers are real. The spine of the work has not changed since nobody was watching.
