Urban News

The flagship. Co-hosted with Lucy Ogunde. Two million-plus Kenyans every week, across TV and social.

Weekdays at 2PM, with prime editions Monday, Wednesday and Friday at 7:30PM EAT. Entertainment, technology, and Kenyan culture, reported in the voice of the country that actually lives it. The bedrock of the channel's youth strategy.

Campus Xposure

The campus tour. Off the desk, onto the lawn.

Student culture, raw field energy, real conversations. Interviews with student leaders, creatives, scientists, and the kids who run the back of every great school. Travel episodes. Live editions. The pulse of a generation, on tape.

The Nairobi Podcast

The deep dive. Long form, on the record.

The creative economy, mental health, lifestyle, and digital culture. Available on Spotify and Amazon Music, in PPP TV production. Rotating co-hosts including Lucy Ogunde, Mary Maina and Rania Biketi.

IV · Founded MMXXV · Co-founder

Urban Gang Tour

Kenya's premier school and campus events company.

Co-built with Lucy Ogunde. Ten battle lanes, a full production crew, a national broadcast at the back of every stage. Singing. Rap. Dance. Spoken word. Poetry. Modelling. News reporting. Public speaking. Art. Even a teachers' competition.

The 2026 schools tour is live — Senior Chief Koinange Girls, Loreto Kiambu, Gathirimu Girls Technical — every stop broadcast on Urban News. The motto: from potential to purpose.

Roylandz Media

The mothership.

A media company that helps brands, events and institutions tell their story with authenticity, strategy, and digital innovation. Strategy, brand voice, social, content production — and lately, the machines: autonomous newsroom and social publishing systems built in-house, running the brands around the clock.
