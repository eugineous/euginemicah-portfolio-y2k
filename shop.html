A note from Eugine

Small runs.
Made on purpose.

Every item in the crown line is produced in a deliberately small run.

The tees and hoodies are sewn in Nairobi. The prints are signed in the studio. The book is a working object, not a souvenir. If you want a piece, write directly. Stock moves the way it moves. The line is always on.

★ Questions ★

Shipping & payment

How do I pay?
M-Pesa, card, or bank transfer. You'll get payment details when you reserve.
Do you ship outside Kenya?
Yes. Countrywide within Kenya and worldwide by DHL Express. Shipping is quoted at reservation.
How long until it arrives?
In-stock items ship within a few days. Pre-orders and made-to-order apparel follow the drop date noted on the item.
Are the prints really signed?
Every print is hand-signed and numbered in editions. The hardcover memoir is signed too.
